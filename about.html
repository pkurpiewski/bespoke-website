About Bespoke

A locally owned commercial cleaning company built on a simple idea: cleaning you never have to think about.

Our Story

Founded to fix an everyday frustration

Bespoke Commercial Services was started in Louisville, Kentucky to do one thing better than the rest: show up, do excellent work, and stay easy to reach. Too many businesses settle for cleaners who are inconsistent and unresponsive. We don't think that's acceptable.

The name says it. We don't sell one-size-fits-all packages — we build a cleaning plan around your space, your hours, and the standards that matter to you. Then we hold our crews to it, visit after visit.

Proudly local. Serving offices, gyms, and stores throughout the Louisville area.
